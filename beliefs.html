Living Doctrine of Unity and Liberation

  • The Path of Love and Compassion

    • "Love one another as I have loved you" -Jesus
    • "The wound is the place where the light enters you" -Rumi
    • Love is the highest law -Unconditional, courageous, and inclusive
    • Extend love to all beings, especially the suffering, forgotten, and feared
    • Heal, forgive, serve, and uplift... not because others deserve it, but because love transforms

  • The Practice of Stillness and Simplicity

    • "Be still, and know..." Psalm 46:10
    • "Do your work, then step back. The only path to serenity." -Laozi
    • Cultivate silence, breath, and presence as sacred acts
    • Detach from greed, power, and statu; embrace simplicity
    • Let go of control -allow life to flow as water through your being

  • The Courage to Question and Seek

    • "The unexamined life is not worth living" -Socrates
    • "Seek and you shall find" -Jesus
    • Truth is alive -it must be sought, not possessed.
    • Ask hard questions, even of the sacred.
    • Do not blindly follow tradition or authority -follow conscience and insight

  • The Sacred Circle of Belonging

    • "All things are our relatives; what we do to everything, we do to ourselves" -Black Elk
    • We are all connected -Human, Animal, Spiirt, Land, and Sky
    • No being stands above another, all beings hold scared purpose
    • Act in ways that preserve balance and honor life's interdepenence

  • The Fire of Justice and Liberation

    • "Blessed are those who hunger and thirst for justice" -Jesus
    • "Be the change you wish to see" -Gandhi
    • Stand with the oppressed, even if it costs you
    • Speak truth to power, but do so with love
    • Freedom is sacred -Not just personal, but collective

  • The Way of Inner Transformation

    • "Die before you die, and find that there is no death" -Rumi
    • The outer world changes, when the inner world awakens
    • Ego must be surrendered, peace comes from within
    • Daily Practice -Prayer, meditation, or silence is you temple

  • The Dance of Paradox

    • "The Tao that can be named is not the Eternal Tao" -Laozi
    • Truth is not a destination -It is a living dance between opposites
    • Hold space for mystery, contradictions, and unkowns
    • Faith is not Belief -it is radical openess to Wonder

  • The Call to Serve

    • "Whoever wants to be great must be servant to all" -Jesus
    • Your gifts are not yours -They belong to the world.
    • Lead by example, not dominance
    • Be a healer, a teacher, and a protector of the vulnerable

  • The Eternal Unity

    • "In truth, we are not seperate. We are drops returning to the ocean" -Rumi
    • All Paths lead to the One -Not a god in a box, but the sacred essence behind All.
    • There is no chosen people -only chosen moments & chosen choices
    • Love is the name of God in every tongue

Closing

Walk gently. Speak Truth. Love fiercly. Surrender often. Listen Deeply. Protect life. Become what you pray for.
